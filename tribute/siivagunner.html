The SiIvaGunner Timeline

A Summary of Events


[summary of events of relevance to SilvaGunner up until today. Refer to this when necessary, keep it in a safe place - V.]

  • 1960
      September 30
      The Flintstones airs on television.
  • 1986
      The Bowflex 2000X, the first product in the Bowflex line, is distributed to the public.
  • 1991
      The first Mr. Rental store opens in Australia.
  • 1992
      7 Grand Dad, a bootleg of The Flintstones: The Rescue of Dino and Hoppy, is released.
  • 1995
      November 17
      "mm2wood.mid" is created, planting the seeds that would later manifest as the Wood Man glitch.
  • 1999
      June 29
      "Smooth" by Santana featuring Rob Thomas of Matchbox Twenty is released. It would later win three Grammy awards.

      November 22
      Donkey Kong 64 is released in North America.
  • 2004
      August 15
      The first episode of LazyTown airs on Television.
  • 2006
      SilvaGunner, a Youtube channel dedicated to uploading video game music, is created.
      TimmyTurnersDad, a similar channel, is also created.
  • 2007
      January 25
      THE iDOLM@STER is released for the Xbox 360.

      April 25
      The first episode of The Nutshack airs on Myx TV.

      May 2
      Sulja Boy releases his hit single "Crank That (Soulja Boy)"

      May 13
      Chad Warden uploads "Why the PS3 is better than the Wii and 360, part 1."
  • 2008
      A pornographic video recording involving an African-American man screaming very loudly is uploaded to the internet.
  • 2010
      SilvaGunner is terminated.

      August
      The Love Live! School idol project begins.
  • 201X
      A young boy from Onett rises from the dead.
  • 2011
      February 7
      TimmyTurnersDad is terminated.

      October
      GilvaSunner, a tribute channel to the original SilvaGunner, is created.

      December 22
      "Snow Halation" by μ's is released.
  • 2013
      June 3
      8-Bit-Beast releases his hit single "Haunted House" (aka "Castle Boss.")
  • 2014
      June 1
      The "Melody of the Heart" episode of Love Live! is aired.

      September 4
      Vinesauce streamer Joel streams 7 Grand Dad.

      October 3
      The LazyTown episode "Robbie's Dream Team" is aired.
  • 2016
      January 1
      "new pokemon battle music LEAKED", by dreammaker222, is uploaded to SoundCloud.

      January 9
      A small meteor crashes near Tokyo Station Marunouchi. The meteor is named GiIvaSunner,
      and the GiIvaSunner YouTube channel, dedicated to high quality rips of video game music, is created.

      January 18
      SoundCloud user crimpsonfang9 has a request. It's "five nights of snop dog".

      March 30
      The first compilation album, GiIvaSunner's Highest Quality Video Game Rips: Volume 1, is released.

      April 1
      GiIvaSunner's Highest Quality Video Game Rips: Volume 2 is released.

      April 6
      Angry Joe hijacks a flight that GiIvaSunner is on, puts out his eye, and puts him in a coma.
      The channel is terminated with no survivors.

      April 12
      GiIvaSunner awakens from his coma and renames himself SiIvaGunner.
      The Metal Gear-themed album, The Phantom Rips: SiIva has come to is released.

      April 19
      Maki Nishikino's birthday. The trend of releasing μ's-themed birthday rips for each idol on begins.

      April 20
      7 Gram Dab, the cannabis-themed album, is released.

      May 7
      The album KORIBALL is released.

      May 8
      7 GRAND MOTHER, the MOTHER/EarthBound-themed album, is released.

      May 17
      Chad Warden notices some discourse within the SiIvaGunner fanbase and tries to quell the flames. He fails.

      May 23
      765 GRAND DAD, the Namco-themed album, is released.

      May 28
      JustinRPG is deployed to SiIvaGunner by an unknown person to further increase the community discourse.

      May 30
      The third compilation album, GiIvaSunner's Highest Quality Video Game Rips: Volume 3 & Knigra, is released.
      SiIvaGunner uploads "Good Night - Undertale" and proceeds to take a brief, week-long nap.

      June 7
      SiIvaGunner wakes up and resumes uploading rips, including "Hyrule Field - The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time."

      June 9
      Nozomi Tojo's birthday: Many Nozomi-themed rips are released on this day to celebrate.

      June 17
      The community discourse reaches critical overload. SiIvaGunner is rebooted, beginning the SiIvaGunner: Rebooted arc. The running gags of the channel are replaced with their parallel universe counterparts.

      June 20
      Father's Day. The first ARG begins.
      Upon its conclusion, it is revealed that The Voice Inside Your Head, a miltidimensional being, orchestrated the reboot.

      June 21
      SiIvaGunner, in his rebooted state, purchases a copy of Mighty No. 9 in order to produce quality rips of its soundtrack.
      Due to the bugginess of the game, the Wood Man glitch gains sentience and Wood Man is born.

      June 24
      Grand Dad 64, an album of Super Mario 64 rips, is silently released to the public.

      June 25
      7 Somari Dad, an album of Sonic the Hedgehog rips, is released to the public.

      July 8
      The second ARG commences.
      The Voice Inside Your Head ends the channel reboot.

      July 9
      The second ARG ends.
      The Voice Inside Your Head reveals that he sent JustinRPG to devastate the original universe.

      Chad Warden sacrifices himself to kill the Voice and save Snow Halation, ending the reboot for good.
      The album SiIvaGunner's Highest Quality Video Game Rips: Volume FOUR HOURS! is released.

      July 11
      SiIvaGunner uploads "Main Theme - Five Nights at Freddy's", fulfilling crimpsonfang9's request.
      SiIvaGunner dedicates many of his rips on this day to Satoru Iwata.

      July 15
      Gangnam Style's birthday. SiIvaGunner makes rips dedicated to it.

      July 16
      "POKEMON GO SONG!!!by MISHA(FOR KIDS) [ORIGINAL]" is released.

      July 22
      Many Sonic rips are uploaded to celebrate Sonic the Hedgehog's 25th anniversary.
      Nico Yazawa-themed rips are uploaded to celebrate her birthday.

      July 28
      Soulja Boy-themed rips are uploaded to celebrate his birthday.

      July 29
      GiIvaSunner's Highest Quality Video Game Rips: Volume 5 is released.

      July 31
      SiIvaGunner ends.

      July 31 [2 hours later]
      SiIvaGunner resumes.

      August 3
      Honoka Kousaka's birthday. Many Honoka-themed rips are uploaded to celebrates.

      August 22
      Heaven Grand Dad, a Rhythm Heaven-themed album, is released.

      August 23
      Kirby Rip Attack, a Kirby-themed album, is released.

      September 2
      Green de la Bean makes his first appearance on his channel.

      il settimo grande padre, an album of the high quality rips of Yoko Shimomura music, is released.

      September 7
      Brazil's Independence Day, Many Brazil-themed rips are uploaded today.

      September 9
      Green de la Bean is killed.
      Many ice-themed rips are uploaded in honor of Cirno from Touhou Project.

      September 10
      SiIvaGunner mourns Green de la Bean's death.

      September 11
      The Jetsons finally debut on SiIvaGunner. Many patriotic, tower-themed and Jetsons-themed rips are uploaded. The Jetsons crash into a tower, and are never seen on the channel again.

      All rips uploaded on this date mysteriously vanish, and no more rips are uploaded for the rest of the day.
      The YouTube channel FlusteredFernando is created, and it reuploads the missing rips.

      September 12
      SiIvaGunner resumes uploading rips, having absolutely no memory of the previous day or the rips he uploaded on it.

      Kotori Minami's birthday. Bird-themed rips are uploaded in celebration.

      September 13
      Anniversary of the release of Super Mario Bros. Many Mario-themed rips are released today.

      September 15
      A large number of MOTHER/EarthBound-themed rips are uploaded, lasting almost the entire day.
      Over 30 Undertale-themed rips are dumped onto the channel in the last hour, celebrating its one year anniversary.

      September 16
      SiIvaGunner attempts to upload a high quality rip of "Vampire Killer" from Castlevania, but Wood Man corrupts it into "Haunted House" (aka "Castle Boss" by 8-Bit Beast.)

      Septemb-ARR the nineteenth
      International Talk Like A Pirate Day, the most swashbucklin' time o' the year'
      Pirate-themed shanties were sung farrrr and wide!

      September 24
      Ooooooooo I Smoke The Weed, I'm Bob Dylan, a Bob Dylan-themed album, is released.

      September 25
      Gurando no Mori, an album of rips from the movie Animal Crossing, is released.
      beatmania IIDX 7 GRAND, an album of rips from BEMANI games, is released.

      September 26
      Crypt of the Necrokazoo, an album of kazoo rips from Crypt of the Necrodancer, is released.

      September 27
      In honour of Joseph Joestar's birthday, the album [7 STAND DAD] is released.
      Upon further inspection, it is revealed to actually be an epic jojoke called JOEJOE'S BIZZAERE JOJOKE.

      September 28
      Quality Sampler, an album of rips from LSD:Dream Emulator, is released.
      SILVA GUNNER BEST OF SOUNDTRACK [B/W] , an album full of songs by Hiroyuki Sawano rearranged in the Pokemon Black and White soundfont, is released.

      September 29
      SiIvaGunner is on his last legs. Thus his swan song featuring the highest quality rips to this date takes place.

      Stagg Street Arrangements is released, an album comprising of arrangements that cover the album Stagg Street Recordings of the band Kara's Flowers.

      CD Grand Beta is released, comprising Genesis arrangements of songs from the game Sonic CD.

      The Phantom Rips: Substinence is released, adding new tracks and remastering some of the old ones.

      The Five Nights at Freddy's Original Soundtrack is released, consisting of tracks from the game Five Nights at Freddy's.

      PC Master Rips, an album of PC-related remixes, is released as an early access album.

      September 30
      SiIvaGunner uploads "the nutshack theme but the first nutshack is replaced with me ending the channel."
      Regular rips come to a close, and the SiIvaGunner channel formally ends.

      October 19
      Wood Man uploads "Wood Man - Mega Man 2" without SiIvaGunner's knowledge.

      October 29
      SiIvaGunner uploads "Title Theme & Ending - 7 Grand Dad."
      Having produced the highest quality rip of his career, he finally goes to sleep.

      November 4-7
      Wood Man uploads Halloween-themed rips to SiIvaGunner's channel.

      November 15
      Wood Man releases the SiIvaGunner Spooktacular Halloween Horror Special: Volume 8-Bit Beast, an album of his Halloween rips.

      November 30
      Wood Man announces that he has been "ordered by Dr. Wily to fight Mega Man" and abandons SiIvaGunner's Twitter and YouTube accounts.

      December 14
      The SiIvaGunner Christmas Comeback Crisis arc begins.

      The Voice Inside Your Head returns, uses SiIvaGunner's computers to release Figments into reality,
      and takes over Grandiose City and SiIvaGunner's YouTube Channel, vowing to destroy all anime.

      December 15
      The Voice Inside Your Head broadcasts a recruitment video ordering his subjects to submit high-quality rips.

      December 25
      Santa Claus's workshop at the North Pole is annihilated by a huge explosion.
  • 2017
      January 9
      The anniversary of the original GiIvaSunner channel...
